## ğŸ” Insights & Interpretation  

- Some games show **low CR to Paid**, which may indicate:
  - weak ad targeting
  - low perceived value of in-game purchases
  - friction in the payment flow  

- **ARPPU differences** between games hint at:
  - content quality and depth
  - value of in-game items / bundles
  - pricing and discount strategy  

- Engagement and cohort charts suggest that:
  - better onboarding and early-game experience can improve retention
  - time-limited offers and events may increase both activity and revenue
